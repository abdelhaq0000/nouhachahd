Delicious Cookie Recipe

Here's a simple recipe to make mouthwatering cookies:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. In a mixing bowl, cream together 1 cup of butter and 1 cup of white sugar until smooth.

3. Beat in 2 eggs and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.

4. Gradually add 2 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Mix well.

5. Stir in 2 cups of chocolate chips (or any other desired mix-ins like nuts or raisins).

6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.

7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown around the edges.
